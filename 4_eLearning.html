Full Project Description

Because these projects proceed from private sector employment, I cannot share all the details. However, this brief example demonstrates a small piece of the visual and interaction design that goes into every full length module I create. Most modules range from 15 to 60 minute learning experiences (although modules for the regulatory health colleges of Ontario have been as long as 90 minutes). Additionally, the eLearning modules designed health colleges of Ontario met the applicable accessibility standards, including full audio narration, keyboard control and high visual contrast. All eLearning modules were created using adult learning principles: namely, they are designed to be problem-based not didactic, self-directed, goal oriented, and practical.
